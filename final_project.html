Not Just A Cartoon: Anime Trends

Introduction

Welcome to "Not Just A Cartoon: Anime Trends", an interactive data Visualization project that dives into the beautiful world of anime. Anime has been a growing industry with a wide variety of shows, genres, studios, stories, characters and much more. Through a series of insightful, engaging, and clear data visualizations, this project explores various aspects of the anime industry, including genre popularity and trends over time, release patterns, audience ratings and the intricate web of anime productions. This project aims at both ends of the spectrum when it comes to its target audience. Whether you're a devoted anime fan or a newcomer, I aim to provide a deeper understanding of how anime has evolved and what factors contribute to the growing appeal. Join me on this data-driven journey to uncover the trends and stories behind your favorite anime series.

Anime Library

Below is a basic anime search library that utilizes the Jikan API, which is an unofficial MyAnimeList API. You can search for any anime that is on the MyAnimeList database. Each anime will be presented with its full name, image, genre, and synopsis.
